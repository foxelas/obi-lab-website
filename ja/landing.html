Innovation and Technology

研究室 The human in the center

Keeping the human in front-center, we explore ways in which biomedical and information engineering can improve our quality of life. Our research branches out in two, specifically in the biomedical and the informatics directions. Work with us, either from the side of medical imaging and computer assisted medical systems, or towards secure social information infrastructure.

Medical Imaging

Positron Emission Tomography (PET), Multispectral Imaging in Gross Pathology, Hyperspectral Imaging in Histology.

Biomedical Analysis

Analysis of time data for diabetes patients, suggestion systems for dietary advice.

Security Systems

Frameworks for the use of My number card or blockchain for the secure transaction of medical information.
