Join us

Information for prospective students

At Obi Laboratory, we are researching new biometric technologies necessary for investigating human biological activities, especially image processing technologies necessary for elucidating biological functions. We are also conducting research on social information system technology necessary for solving various social issues and building social infrastructure that allows users to safely use medical information.

Obi Laboratory belongs to the Institute of Innovative Research of our university. We accept undergraduate and graduate students of the School of Engineering's Information and Communications Engineering department to conduct education and research. In addition, since we are conducting research in collaboration with faculty members belonging to the Advanced Research Center for Social Information Science And Technology, you can select a research theme from a wide range of themes related to the medical and the social information fields.
